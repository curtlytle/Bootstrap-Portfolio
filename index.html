About Me

I was raised in Pueblo Colorado. I graduated from BYU in 1992 with a BS in Mechanical Engineering with an emphasis in technology. I married my sweetheart in 1988 and have two children


My first job out of college was working for Bath Iron Works (BIW), in Maine. BIW built Navy destroyers and I was employed in their IT department writing supporting software for the ship designers. That is how I started my career in the software industry, and have been developing software ever since. Currently my goal is to become a true full stack web developer, where most of my career has been in the Java stack, backend frameworks with JDBC, Hibernate, Servlets and Struts frameworks.


I am almost complete with a University of Utah full stack bootcamp course. We are learning the MERN stack, along with frameworks like Sequelize, Mongoose, API Rest service architectures, and several more.


My current position is doing core Java, with Swing and Hibernate.
